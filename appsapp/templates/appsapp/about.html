About This System

This is a minimalistic webapp created with django in order to practice and showcase my skills. As admin, I can add my job applications to the system, including relevant documents like CV, cover letter, transcript of records etc. Recruiters and managers from different companies can see all the details and documents of the applications to their company, and they can also change the status of the applications or the "next step" field. Recruiters can also see that applications to other companies exist and what their status is, but they cannot see the position or the company, or the documents of those applications.
